1.How to draw horizontal lines in the terminal using bash shell:

1.a.: printf '%*s\n' "${COLUMNS:-$(tput cols)}" '' | tr ' ' -
1.b.: printf "%`tput cols`s"|sed "s/ /_/g"
1.c.: printf -v _hr "%*s" $(tput cols) && echo ${_hr// /${1--}}
